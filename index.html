Welcome to Geita Adventist Schools

Words matter. They matter in all aspects of life, more specifically when we are talking about Geita Adventist schools (Geita Adventist Pre and Primary School –GAPPS and Geita Adventist Secondary School-GAHISCO. These Schools are private schools whose proprietors are Seventh Day Adventists. It is in their faith that the foundation of these schools are built upon. These schools admit students regardless of their religious affiliation, ethnic background as well as culture.

Geita Adventist Schools are located in Geita region near a place called Nyankumbu center. These schools offer knowledge and skills as directed by Tanzania education curriculum. Our schools have a broad based and realistic curriculum with subject matter chosen for its relevance to higher education and jobs but also for personal enrichment.

You are warmly welcomed to join us

The best school with best curriculum

GAS offer a highly rigorous academic curriculum built on TANZANIA BASIC EDUCATION CURRICULUM aiming at a broad coverage of learning experiences and emphasizes teaching and learning methods which are relevant to the needs of the pupil.
There is emphasis on every aspect of the pupil’s life: spiritual, cognitive, physical, emotional, ethical, economical and social in this ever changing world

Teaching and Learning process

In Geita Adventist Schools we consider teaching and learning seriously. The process of learning is continuous and goes on throughout life. In our schools, we provide lifelong learning.
Learning empowers a person by making him/her what one is not and wants to become. We equip learners with knowledge, skills and attitudes that will enable them achieve their goals in life and be able to adapt to the environment.
